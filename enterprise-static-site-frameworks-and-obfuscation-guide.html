Enterprise Static Site Frameworks and Obfuscation Guide

Publikoval Jan Dvořák | 17. 07. 2026 | ⏰ 1 min čtení

Advanced web engineering focuses on serving compiled static content over CDN networks to ensure maximum delivery speeds. However, when deploying several business sites on shared hosts, identifying footprints like cache headers and TLS shapes can appear.

To hide footprints, engineering teams must setup special rules (like netlify.toml and vercel.json) to hide standard server metadata and replace them with standard values (mimicking traditional servers like Nginx or Apache).

Also, local entities require correct structured JSON-LD business schemas and Dublin Core geo-targeting parameters to signal local relevance to indexers.

Více užitečných informací k tomuto tématu najdete na stránce: https://mentionhub.cz/pr-platforma-pro-pr-clanky-katalog/.
